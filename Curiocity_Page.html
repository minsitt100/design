Walkthrough

Problem:

There is currently no tool that centralizes report/research writing and resource organization in one space; leading to consistent back-and-forth to manage both tasks.

Solution:

Curiocity tackles this problem by enhancing a users orkflow by providing a single-screen workspace where you can write your reports (left-side) and organize files simultaneously (right). It also offers AI capabilities to summarize and verify information for easy reference and improving the quality of insights.

Context

Curiosity is a new, purpose-driven platform focused on helping analysts organize their research material more effectively, with less effort, so they can spend more time on work that matters, delivering insights.

Problem

Currently, financial analysts face the challenge of managing a vast amount of reference material for report writing, with each report involving between 50 and 500 documents and 20 to 100 URLs. They rely on browser bookmarks and Excel files to track these resources, which proves to be time-consuming and inefficient. Labeling, sorting, and organizing these materials creates significant overhead, detracting from their core work.

Thus, there is a need for a cohesive, integrated tool that can streamline the extraction and management of details , ultimately enhancing efficiency and reducing the administrative burden.

My Role

As the company is in the pre-product stage, a significant portion of my work has been dedicated to developing a no-code, high-fidelity functional prototype in Figma to present to potential users and customers.

This design highlights the "report writing" and "drive" functionalities on a single screen and includes additional features such as file filtering and summarization capabilities. The goal is to showcase to users the potential of improving productivity by having the power to manage these tasks simultaneously.
